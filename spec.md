# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Fix all text contrast issues across every section of the Pro Fix Handyman site so all text is clearly legible against its background.

**Planned changes:**
- Audit text contrast in Navigation, Hero, Services, About, Contact, and Footer sections
- Fix any text colors that blend into their backgrounds by adjusting foreground colors, adding text shadows, or strengthening background overlays
- Ensure orange-on-white and white-on-dark color combinations maintain strong contrast
- Make all body text, headings, labels, and UI elements crisp and readable throughout the page

**User-visible outcome:** Every piece of text on the site is clearly readable with no faded, washed-out, or blended text in any section.
